The Problem

Seeking help online is a chore. Q&A websites currently don’t provide the necessary resources for seeking or providing help online. Text alone isn’t sufficient to communicate problems.

The Solution

HALP solves this by allowing users to visually communicate the problem. People can snap a photo of their issue, annotate it, and upload it. Others can jump in and share their expertise through photos of their own.

Features

SNAP IT

Snap a photo and upload it as a post or reply

ANNOTATE IT

Annotate the photo to better express the problem or solution

POST IT

Post problems to boards for others to jump in and help
